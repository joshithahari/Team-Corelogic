🛡️ GigShield – AI-Powered Income Protection Platform
🚀 Overview
GigShield is an innovative AI-powered parametric insurance platform designed to protect the weekly earnings of gig workers such as food delivery partners (Zomato, Swiggy, etc.) across India.
Gig workers are highly vulnerable to external disruptions that are beyond their control. Events like heavy rainfall, extreme heat, or sudden curfews can completely halt their ability to work, leading to instant income loss.
👉 GigShield solves this problem by offering a smart, automated income protection system that detects such events and provides instant payouts without manual claims.
________________________________________
🌍 Problem Statement
Gig workers face multiple real-world challenges:
•	❌ No fixed salary or income stability
•	🌧️ Work disruption due to extreme weather
•	🚫 App shutdowns during strikes/curfews
•	🕒 Delays and complexity in traditional insurance claims
👉 Existing insurance solutions:
•	Are slow
•	Require manual verification
•	Do not cover short-term income loss
________________________________________
💡 Our Solution
GigShield introduces a Parametric Insurance Model where:
•	📡 External events are automatically monitored
•	⚡ Claims are instantly triggered
•	💰 Payouts are credited without user intervention
This creates a zero-friction insurance experience for gig workers.
________________________________________
🧭 Methodology & System Flow
 
The system operates in three major stages:
1️⃣ Onboarding Flow
•	User enters basic details (Name, Platform, City)
•	Minimal friction ensures quick access
•	System prepares user profile for risk evaluation
________________________________________
2️⃣ AI Risk Profiling Engine
•	AI evaluates:
o	🌦️ Weather patterns (rainfall, heatwaves)
o	🚨 Social disruptions (strikes, curfews)
o	📊 Historical city-level data
•	Generates:
o	📈 Risk Score
o	💸 Weekly Premium (dynamic pricing)
________________________________________
3️⃣ Real-Time Claim Automation
•	Continuously monitors external conditions
•	When thresholds are met:
o	⚡ Trigger detected (e.g., Heatwave > 45°C)
o	🤖 Claim auto-generated
o	💰 Instant payout credited
•	Updates:
o	📜 AI Claim Log
o	💼 Protected Earnings
